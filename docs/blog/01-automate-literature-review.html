Why Automate?

A typical PhD student spends months on their first literature review. Most tasks are mechanical: searching databases, downloading PDFs, extracting key information.

Step 1: Batch Search and Download from arXiv

The arXiv API lets you search and download programmatically. No clicking, no captchas.

Step 2: Extract Text and Figures from PDFs

Use PyMuPDF to pull text and embedded images from PDFs in seconds.

Step 3: Organize by Year and Author

Rename PDFs consistently and sort into folders automatically.

The Bottom Line

Automating your literature workflow saves hundreds of hours over a PhD.
